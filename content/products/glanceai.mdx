Experience the future of web browsing with GlanceAI. Our innovative browser extension transforms how you interact with web content, making information gathering and research more efficient than ever. Using advanced AI technology, GlanceAI provides instant summaries, key points, and relevant insights for any webpage you visit.

## Key Features

### AI-Powered Summaries
Get instant, accurate summaries of any webpage using advanced AI technology

### Interactive Chat
Engage with our AI to ask questions about any webpage content

### Key Points Extraction
Automatically identify and highlight the most important information

### Smart Navigation
Discover related content and resources with intelligent suggestions
